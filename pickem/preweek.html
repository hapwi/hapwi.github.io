How it works

$180 entry fee, payable to (@pblang) on Venmo.

Registration: Upon successful registration, participants will receive an email by 09/04/2023 containing a unique username and password. These credentials will be required for submitting picks each week and are implemented to prevent unauthorized entries after week 1.

Weekly Competitions: Each week, participants' picks will be evaluated to determine the number of correct choices. The weekly winner will receive $5 for each entry in the contest. For instance, if 10 participants are in the contest, the weekly winner will be awarded $50.

Season Championship: At the conclusion of the 18-week season, the cumulative wins will be tallied. The individual with the highest total wins across the season will be awarded the remaining buy-in fees. As an example, if the contest has 10 participants, resulting in a total buy-in of $1800, and the weekly prize is $50 ($50 x 18 = $900), the season champion will receive the balance of $900.
